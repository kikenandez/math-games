hop the pyramid · balance the puzzle tiles to zero

Hop the pyramid with arrow keys or WASD. Visit every tile to clear it Q*bert-style. A few interior tiles are math tiles with a value — descending hops (SE/SW) add +1, ascending hops (NE/NW) subtract −1. Bring every math tile to 0. Dodge the red balls tumbling down from the apex, and watch for the snake that hatches at the top and chases you.
